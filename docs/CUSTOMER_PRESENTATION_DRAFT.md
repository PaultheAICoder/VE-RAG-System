## How It Works

### Step 1: Upload Your Documents

Drop your files into VaultIQ — policies, certificates, loss runs, endorsements, CC&Rs, everything. Any format: PDF, Word, Excel, scanned images.

### Step 2: AI Reads, Extracts, and Organizes Everything

Our AI reads every page, extracts every important detail, and stores it two ways:

**Structured insurance database** — hard facts go into organized tables, just like a spreadsheet:
- **Policy details** — carrier, policy number, limits, deductibles, premiums, dates
- **Coverage breakdowns** — per-occurrence, aggregate, sublimits, endorsements
- **Claims history** — dates, amounts, status, descriptions from loss runs
- **Property data** — units, construction, values from appraisals and reserve studies
- **Compliance requirements** — insurance minimums from CC&Rs and bylaws

**Document intelligence** — the full content of every document is indexed and searchable, so VaultIQ can find answers buried in legal language, exclusion clauses, and CC&R sections.

### Step 3: Ask Questions, Get Answers

Type any question in plain English. VaultIQ checks the database first for an exact answer, then searches your documents if needed:

- **Structured facts** (limits, carriers, premiums) come from the database — instant and exact
- **Analytical questions** (CC&R interpretation, coverage analysis) come from AI-powered document search — thorough and cited
- **Combined answers** — some questions use both: "Do our limits meet the CC&R requirements?" pulls your current limits from the database AND the requirement language from the CC&R document

## FAQ

**Q: Can I try it with just one property first?**
A: Yes. Upload one property's documents and start using it immediately. Add more properties at any pace.

**Q: What if the AI gets something wrong?**
A: For factual questions like limits and premiums, answers come directly from a structured database — not AI interpretation — so they're exact. For analytical questions, every answer includes the source document and page so you can verify. If the system isn't confident in an answer, it flags it for your review rather than guessing. Your team can correct any answer, and the system learns from those corrections.

**Q: Do I need to organize my files before uploading?**
A: No. Drop everything in and the AI classifies and organizes it. Policies, loss runs, certificates, CC&Rs — it figures out what's what.

**Q: Can my staff use it too?**
A: Yes. Unlimited users. You control who sees which properties through tag-based access control.

**Q: What about sensitive client data?**
A: With either option, your data is never shared with other customers. With **Standard**, your agency gets its own dedicated instance — your own database, your own application, completely isolated. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest in US data centers. Sensitive fields like SSNs and account numbers receive additional encryption. With **Enterprise**, everything stays on your hardware in your office — nothing leaves your network. Either way, the AI document reading step processes content securely and doesn't retain your data externally.

**Q: Which option should I start with?**
A: Most agencies start with **Standard** (cloud-hosted). It's live same-day, delivers the highest answer quality, and requires no hardware investment. Your data is fully isolated in your own dedicated instance — it's not a shared environment. You can always move to Enterprise later if your data requirements change. We recommend starting with one property on Standard to see the value before making any long-term decisions.

**Q: How is this different from my agency management system?**
A: They work together. Your AMS (Epic, HawkSoft, Applied) tracks transactions — policies bound, commissions earned, renewals due. VaultIQ understands the *content* of your documents. It reads the actual policy, extracts the actual limits, builds a structured insurance database, and answers questions about what's actually covered. Think of VaultIQ as the knowledge layer that sits alongside your AMS — it doesn't replace your workflow, it makes your team faster within it.

**Q: Can VaultIQ be customized for how my agency works?**
A: Yes. Because each agency gets its own dedicated instance, we can configure VaultIQ to match your workflow — custom document categories, agency-specific compliance rules, preferred report formats, and branding. Your instance is yours.
